Our Story

VKPT - Vios Keli Pantai Timur

The group named Vios Keli Pantai Timur was established in May 2023 in Kuantan, Pahang. Its primary goal is to unite owners of the Vios NCP150 (Vios Keli) across the East Coast states of Pahang, Kelantan, and Terengganu. The group serves as a platform for members to share ideas on car modifications, exchange experiences regarding common issues faced by Vios users, and collaborate on finding solutions. Guided by the slogan, "It's not only about cars, it's about friendship," the group fosters a sense of community and brotherhood among its members.
